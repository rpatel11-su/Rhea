Shark Week on Discovery Channel!

Shark Week is a 7 Day TV Special that Happens Every Summer

The Discovery Channel has been putting out new specials on different types of sharks every year, many specials follow marine biologists to the middle of the ocean on small boats to lure sharks out of the ocean.

Air Jaws

Air Jaws is a popular special that airs during shark week. It shows shark breaches. A breach is when a Great White Shark jumps all the way out of the water for food. It is quite a majestic site to see!
