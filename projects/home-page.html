Shark week is a week-long phenomenon on the Discovery Channel

Shark week is nationally televised event that lasts a week long and has been an annual occurence since 1988. Each year the Discovery Channel puts out new specials and interviews professionals about many different types of sharks. On this website, I will be discussing sharks at great lengths.
